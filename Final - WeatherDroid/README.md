# Weather Droid

WeatherDroid is a mobile app that aims to present weather forecasts for major cities in the country. 

This app implements the following functionalities:
* Present forecasts through a listing of cities with filter by country;
* Present the detailed forecasts for a city (current day and next 4 days);
* Present the forecasts on the map;
* Present the forecasts for a previously chosen city or for the current location of the device through a Widget;
* Choose the frequency of information refresh (by World Weather Online) with minimum and maximum limits;
* Choose which location (from the list or current device location) to display forecasts in the Widget;
